Homemade Pumpkin Spice Latte

Home

Prep and Cook Time

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
5 mins 5 mins 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso (or 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee)
    • For the espresso: If you’re in a pinch or don’t have any espresso, feel free to use 1/2 cup of freshly brewed coffee. I recommend making it a bit stronger than you normally do.
  • 1 cup milk of choice
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
    • For the pumpkin pie spice: If you don’t have any pre-mixed pumpkin pie spice, feel free to add 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg, 1/8 teaspoon ginger, and 1/8 teaspoon of ground cloves or allspice.
  • 1-2 tbsp pure maple syrup
    • For the sweetener: I like to use a little pure maple syrup because it adds even more of that cozy fall flavor, but feel free to use honey or even brown sugar.
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the milk in a small pot over medium-low heat until hot, but not boiling.
  2. Make espresso or strong coffee, pour into a mug.
  3. Mix in pumpkin puree, maple syrup, pumpkin pie spice, and vanilla.
  4. Froth the heated milk for 30 seconds and pour over the pumpkin mixture.
  5. Top with whipped cream and extra pumpkin spice if desired. Enjoy!
