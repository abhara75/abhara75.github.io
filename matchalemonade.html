Matcha Lemonade

Home

Prep and Cook Time

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
2 mins 2 mins 4 mins

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup diced strawberries, fresh or frozen but thawed
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • 1/5 cup water
  • 1 tbsp matcha
  • Ice (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by mashing the strawberries in a bowl with maple syrup.
  2. Place the strawberries into the glass, along with the lemon juice, water, and ice.
  3. Mix the matcha powder in a small bowl with warm water.
  4. Now pour the matcha into the glass and mix until everything is well combined. Enjoy!
